This is my favorite place throughout the trip -- The Alhambra Palace.
It is one of the best preserved Islamic Palaces and witnessed the prosperity of the Nastrid period.
Thankfully, it was protected and preserved by the monarchs even after the Catholic conquest of Granada.

If you visit Gaudi's works in Barcelona, you will notice that he was heavily influenced by the Moorish patterns of stone carving and arch design; this is especially evident in the Sagrada Familia.
If you plan to visit Granada in the summer, definitely try Arab baths and Iberian ham with melon!
